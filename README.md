# Excel-Projects-Hub
As part of my data analytics learning journey, I recently learned how to analyze Hospital Emergency Room (ER) data and transform it into an interactive dashboard that provides meaningful insights for better decision-making.
## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/sonukumar9557/Excel-Projects-Hub/blob/main/Hospital%20(version%201)%20(version%202).xlsb.xlsx">Dataset view</a>
📌 Dashboard Highlights
🔹 Total Patients – Displays the total number of patients who visited the emergency room.
🔹 Average Wait Time – Measures the average time patients waited before being attended to.
🔹 Patient Satisfaction Score – Tracks patient satisfaction based on feedback.
🔹 Patient Admission Status – Shows how many patients were admitted versus not admitted.
🔹 Patient Age Distribution – Groups patients into different age categories to analyze age-wise ER visits.
🔹 Timeliness Analysis – Measures the percentage of patients who were seen within 30 minutes, helping evaluate emergency response efficiency.
🔹 Gender Analysis – Displays the distribution of patients by gender.
🔹 Department Referrals – Identifies which hospital departments received the highest number of patient referrals, helping understand the most frequently utilized specialties.
